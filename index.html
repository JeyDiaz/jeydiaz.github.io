About Me.

I am a passionate software engineer with a strong foundation in developing innovative and user-friendly digital solutions. My expertise spans responsive web design, where I leverage technologies like HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to create seamless experiences across devices. I have also contributed to advancing online education by designing interactive platforms using modern web technologies, ensuring an engaging learning environment for users. My skills extend to video game development, where I have brought creative visions to life through 2D platformers and immersive 3D first-person shooters using the Unity engine. Whether it's crafting captivating interfaces or implementing intricate game mechanics, I thrive on turning ideas into reality.

Beyond front-end development, I have a solid track record in backend systems and data analytics. My work includes creating secure and efficient login systems for e-commerce platforms using PHP, as well as developing robust point-of-sale systems in C++ that streamline inventory and transaction management. Additionally, I have hands-on experience in cloud data analytics with Google Cloud, where I optimized the processing of large datasets for enhanced business efficiency. My passion for learning and problem-solving drives me to deliver impactful projects that blend technical precision with innovative design.
